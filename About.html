Hilary Tsai is a novice motion designer and junior developer based in Portland, Oregon. She has a BA in English and a BFA in Video & Sound from Pacific Northwest College of Art, and is most interested in the topic of "visual music." Her senior thesis was a 3D projection-mapped tribute to rock band, Queen which paired the group's greatest hits with custom-designed imagery. Hilary also has a love-hate relationship with technnology. In the middle of 2018, she became a student at The Tech Academy of Portland, where she will soon earn a junior developer certification.
